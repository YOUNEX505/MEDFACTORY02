Our story

Training products that prepare you for real patients.

MedFactory helps dental students across Egypt improve practical skills through realistic, faculty-aligned training tools — before the pressure of clinic begins.

Brand story

Born from the gap between lectures and clinical confidence.

Every dental student knows the feeling: you understand the theory, but the first time you prep a tooth or negotiate a canal, everything feels new. The training models matter. And finding the right simulation products shouldn't be another stress.

MedFactory started when we saw students juggling orders — a dummy head from one place, endo teeth from another, casts that didn't match their module needs. We built one destination for everything practical training requires, curated for how dental students actually learn.

Today, students from Cairo to Aswan order from MedFactory because we speak their language: semesters, modules, practice lists, and the quiet anxiety before clinical years. We're not a clinic supplier or equipment company. We're built for you — the student who needs to practice.
